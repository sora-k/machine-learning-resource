Bayes theorem

  • P(A and B) = P(A)P(B|A) = P(B)P(A|B)
  • P(A|B) = P(A)P(B|A) / P(B)
  • P(Hypothesis | Evidence) = P(Hypothesis)P(Evidence | Hypothesis) / P(Evidence)
  • (posterior) = (prior)(likelyhood) / (normalizing constant)
